ACME RETAIL LOGISTICS AND REFUND POLICY MANUAL
1. REFUND WINDOWS: All standard product returns must be initiated within 30 days of the delivery timestamp.
2. PREMIUM MEMBERSHIP PRIVILEGES: VIP Tier members receive an extended 60-day return window and zero restocking fees.
3. PACKAGING RESTRICTIONS: Items must be returned in their original box packaging. A 15% restocking fee is applied if the box is damaged or missing.
4. COURIER ROUTING: International shipments to Central Europe are routed exclusively via DHL Express. Domestic US shipping uses FedEx Home Delivery.
